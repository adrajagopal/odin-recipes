My Hash Brown Recipe

Description

If you're making a frittata, as we did in our Frittata Recipe, it's great to have a side to go along with it. I usually use potatoes as my side dish for breakfast. I could do this from scratch, but I'm feeling lazy today so I'm just going to go with a frozen bag of potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of OreIda has browns
  • salt
  • ground black pepper
  • smoked paprika
  • chili powder
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • ketchup, or whatever sauce you like to eat hash browns with

Steps

  1. Heat oil in the pan
  2. Add the potatoes
  3. Once the potatoes are decently cooked, add the seasonings
  4. Cook until browned and crispy, stirring occasionally

Shape into pancakes, let cool and enjoy!
