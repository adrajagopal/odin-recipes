My Dosa Recipe

Description

Have you ever actually tried to make dosa batter in your studio apartment, without a grinder? No? OK, then. Ideally, you'd run out and get some premade batter from the grocery store, but it's late and you want dosa now. OK, then.

Over to Doordash.

Ingredients

  • 1 mobile phone
  • 1 Doordash app w/ account created

Steps

  1. Order from Doordash
  2. Regret ordering dosa online, because you know it's going to be soft and cold by the time you get it
  3. Receive your order, and stick it in the toaster oven to see if that helps
  4. It doesn't
  5. Eat the whole thing anyway and go to bed upset

This was your fault.
